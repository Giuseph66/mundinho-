- `dagger.svg` / `dagger.png`: "Plain dagger" by Lorc, CC BY 3.0, https://game-icons.net/1x1/lorc/plain-dagger.html
- `firearm.svg` / `firearm.png`: "Pistol gun" by John Colburn, CC BY 3.0, https://game-icons.net/1x1/john-colburn/pistol-gun.html
